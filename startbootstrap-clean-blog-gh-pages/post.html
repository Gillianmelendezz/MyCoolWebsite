Strong. Independent. HardWorker. This is Me.

We only get one life, might as well make the most out of it Posted by Gillian Melendez on May 07, 2023

I have stories to tell, people to meet, and places to see. I cannot wait to see what awaits me beyond Gonzaga.

From coding robots to writing news articles, my interests go beyond the horizon in discovering what path I should take in life.

I love having fun. I love beach trips. I love eating. I love this amazingly confusing earth we call home.

Being half Italian half Mexican has its perks, as I get to share amazing food and great experiences from both sides of the world. Family means everything to me.

Friendships are the way to a good life. I have memories that I will never forget from having hometown friends to meeting new people from all over the world at Gonzaga.

Who I Am

I am learning every day Who I am and who I want to be. My family has shaped me into becoming a strong and independent woman. I love putting in the effort to see a beautiful outcome.

Working in the restaurant business has taught me that patience and guidance are very important. I know how to communicate with all types of different people which sets me aside from others. You will never see me without a smile on my face. (Maybe if I'm hungry, then the smile will be not as big!)

Every day may not be a great day, but every bad day will make you love and appreciate the good ones.

Being a part of a competitive robotics team has made me who I am today. Traveling globally and placing top 16 out of 80 teams has made me strong. Winning first place is something I will never forget, and I have to thank my amazing robotics coach, Sheri Gundlach who is someone I look up to.

Why I do what I do

I just want to have the best life and make every day mean something. Something I have learned while being at GU is time is fast... and does not slow down. I want to make the most out of every day.

My campus is important to me. Spokane looks different than California, but I was meant to leave home to find my independence.

Beach trips, to Disneyland, to amazing meals and memories made, growing up has been a life of its own thanks to my family. I have been given the tools I need to fly and make a life of my own, as I will never forget where I came from, and the lessons I have learned.

I am Gillian Melendez, a writer, food-lover, and a ready-to-see-the-world girl. I'm coming, so please, look out.
